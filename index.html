Schedule

2018 Workshop Itinerary. A four day extravaganza !

  • Wednesday 8a - 11a

    Big Data Breakfast

    Jackson State University’s College of Science, Engineering and Technology is forging ahead with its focus on Big Data, a field positioned to solve global challenges and create intelligent solutions. With the emergence of personalized medicine, this focus is critical for achieving more precise modeling of complex diseases.

  • Thursday 2p - 5p

    Phase One Linux/Apache/MySQL

    LAMP is an archetypal model of web service stacks, named as an acronym of the names of its original four open-source component; the Linux operating system, the Apache HTTP Server, the MySQL relational database management system, and the PHP programming language. Phase One covers the first three.

  • Friday 11a - 1p

    Engineering Luncheon

    The Luncheon’s main purpose is to serve as a networking opportunity for students and professionals. The event will have a keynote speaker, a chance to learn more about engineering at Jackson State, and an opportunity to meet with students from different engineering disciplines.

  • Saturday 2p - 5p

    Phase Two Python/PHP/PERL

    Phase Two examines the three P's. All these three languages are dynamic and powerful in their own way and can be used in different scenarios, but they are most commonly used to build Web applications and for scripting.

  • LAMP
    Finale
    Sat @ 7pm
